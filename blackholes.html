// FIRST CAPTURED OBSERVATION: EHT M87

EHT M87, known as the first black hole ever grasped by mankind's observation, but will not be the last.

Black holes are mass giants that pull anything and everything towards their core, not even light itself can outrun their force.

The Horizon—can be seen as an effect of warping light located at the edges of the black hole, a clear line between space and “The Point Of No Return.”

The Point Of No Return—the area after you pass The Horizon, the abyss we all know and fear, which is theorized as “Nothing goes in and nothing comes back out.”
