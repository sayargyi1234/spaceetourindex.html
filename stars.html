STARS

Stars tend to have an unimaginable force of gravity and size compared to mere planets. They produce their own light source, making them luminous objects powered by internal fuel.

When their time ends, they exit with a "Bang"—a supernova bright enough to outshine a billion stars, leaving behind colorful patterns of energy and heat before revealing a dwarf remnant.

Comparison 03.B

PLANETS

We live on a planet: an oval-shaped solid mass with the rare ability to retain life. Most possess a core millions of times hotter than their surface.

With their own unique pull of gravity and environment, they are exquisite pieces of cosmic art for us to witness.
